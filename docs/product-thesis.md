# Product Thesis

## Simple thesis

Health portals show information.

CareBridge turns information into action.

## Product identity

CareBridge Canada is the patient-owned action layer between healthcare visits.

It is not a replacement for existing healthcare systems. Existing portals and clinical systems already store or show records.

CareBridge sits beside and above those systems as a continuity layer that helps patients understand, organize, translate, track, and communicate their care.

## Main loop

CareBridge is built around one repeated care loop:

1. Before the visit: prepare.
2. After the visit: understand.
3. Between visits: follow through.
4. Next visit: explain what changed.

## Product promise

CareBridge helps every patient carry:

- a memory
- a translator
- an organizer
- an advocate
- a follow-up system

in their pocket.

## North Star question

Can CareBridge help patients not lose the thread of their care?
